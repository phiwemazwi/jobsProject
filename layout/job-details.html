Job Description

As a Front-End Developer, you will be at the forefront of web development, transforming design concepts into pixel-perfect realities. Your expertise in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript will empower you to build visually stunning and highly responsive user interfaces. You will collaborate closely with our design team, bridging the gap between creativity and functionality.

Responsibilities

As a Front-end Developer, you will work within a Delivery Team fused with UX, engineering, product and data talent.

  • Ensure cross-browser compatibility and mobile responsiveness of web applications.
  • Conduct thorough testing and debugging
  • Ensure the accessibility and usability of web interfaces, following best practices
  • Stay updated on emerging front-end technologies and industry trends
  • Participate in code reviews and provide constructive feedback
Qualification
  • BIT National University course complete.
  • 3 or more years of professional design experience
  • have already graduated or are currently in any year of study
Skill & Experience
  • Understanding of key Design Principal
  • Proficiency With HTML, CSS, Bootstrap (Required)
  • Experience designing and developing responsive design websites
  • Angular
